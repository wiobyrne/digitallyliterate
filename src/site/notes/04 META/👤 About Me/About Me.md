# About Me

Hi, I'm **Ian O'Byrne**. I'm an educator, researcher, and lifelong learner thinking about the places where literacy, technology, and humanity intersect.

![point 1.jpg](/img/user/04%20META/%F0%9F%94%97%20Assets/point%201.jpg)

## What I do

I teach, write, and build frameworks for how we read, write, and participate in digital spaces. Some of my foundational work includes:

- Co-authoring **Mozilla's Web Literacy Framework**
- Co-writing **NCTE's Definition of Literacy in the Digital Age**
- Research with the **New Literacies Research Lab**
- Directing the **Initiative for Literacy in the Digital Age**

These days I'm especially interested in AI literacy, information integrity, digital sovereignty, and what it looks like to build communities around trust rather than extraction.
