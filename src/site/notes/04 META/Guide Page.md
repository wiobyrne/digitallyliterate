# Guide Page

Welcome to the behind-the-scenes tour of my digital garden! Here's how I organize and grow ideas in this space.

## 🌱 Seed Notes
- **Purpose**: Capture initial thoughts or sparks of inspiration.
- **Example**: A single question or insight, stored for future exploration.
- **Location**: [CREATE > Notes > Seeds](#seeds-inbox)

## 🌿 Plant Notes
- **Purpose**: Develop connections and add context to ideas.
- **Example**: Expanding a seed by linking related notes or adding key insights.
- **Location**: [CREATE > Notes > Plants](#plants-inbox)

## 🌳 Evergreen Notes
- **Purpose**: Refined and complete thoughts, ready for long-term use.
- **Example**: A well-developed essay or thoroughly explained concept.
- **Location**: [CREATE > Notes > Evergreens](#evergreens-inbox)

## 📂 Folder Overview
### CONSUME
- **Purpose**: Intake of raw material (books, articles, podcasts, etc.).
- **Includes**: Highlights, summaries, and fleeting thoughts.

### CREATE
- **Purpose**: Space for developing and organizing ideas.
- **Includes**: Seeds, Plants, and Evergreens.

### CURATE
- **Purpose**: Build connections through MOCs (Maps of Content).
- **Includes**: Notes linking related ideas or exploring broader themes.

## 🔍 Quick Tips
- Use tags to track note progress: `#seed`, `#plant`, `#evergreen`.
- Review unprocessed notes regularly to keep ideas flowing.
