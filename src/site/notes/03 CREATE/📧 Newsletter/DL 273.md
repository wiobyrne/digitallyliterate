WELCOME

Shocked, but not surprised

A lot has happened in the world of technology and education since our last issue. A lot of news that we should cover. There are times that a story eclipses all others and I need to try and unpack all of the angles. Sadly, the events of January 6th, 2021 require our full examination. As regular readers of this newsletter, we are shocked...but not surprised at what occurred.

This week I worked on the following:

* Technology is a Tool, Not the Strategy (https://wiobyrne.com/technology-is-a-tool-not-the-strategy/) - When we're looking for new tools, we should focus on solving problems by considering interest, focus, and attention.

* Considerations of Death (https://wiobyrne.com/considerations-of-death/) - It is interesting to watch our considerations of death and the changes brought about by the influx of technology and social networks.

* Before You Post that Hot Take (https://wiobyrne.com/hot-take/) - Seek first to understand, then be understood.
