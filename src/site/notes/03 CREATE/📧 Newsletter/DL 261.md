### [QAnon and the Attention Economy](https://www.buzzfeednews.com/article/janelytvynenko/qanon-families-friends)

For those of you that do not spend their time deep in the online wormhole of conspiracy and misinformation threads, you may not know about [QAnon](https://abcnews.go.com/Politics/men-qanon/story?id=73046374). QAnon is a far-right conspiracy theory alleging that a cabal of Satan-worshiping pedophiles running a global child sex-trafficking ring is plotting against President Donald Trump.

[Whitney Phillips](https://twitter.com/wphillips49) on how we need to talk about how a person's existing worldview feeds into and is fed by recommendation algorithms. This is how the attention economy has become possible, profitable, and untouchable.

QAnon seems to be [rebranding](https://www.nbcnews.com/tech/tech-news/qanon-leaders-look-rebrand-after-tech-crack-downs-n1241125) as they get more attention.

### [Telling the Truth About Slavery Is Not 'Indoctrination'](https://www.theatlantic.com/ideas/archive/2020/09/real-stakes-fight-over-history/616455/)

[Clint Smith](https://www.clintsmithiii.com/) on how our country is made better, not worse, by young people reckoning with the full legacy of the institution.

Such reckoning better prepares them to make sense of how our country has come to be, and how to build systems and institutions predicated on justice rather than oppression. Nothing is more patriotic than that.

### [Whose Anger Counts?](https://bostonreview.net/gender-sexuality/whitney-phillips-whose-anger-counts)

[Whitney Phillips](https://twitter.com/wphillips49) on how cancel culture can go wrong. But that doesn't mean the objections of far-right trolls and social justice activists should be mistaken for having equal worth.

If you truly want to do something about cancel culture, take the radical step of doing what you do for everyone else. See them.

### [Raising Good Gamers](https://clalliance.org/publications/raising-good-gamers-envisioning-an-agenda-for-diversity-inclusion-and-fair-play/)

In February 2020, leading researchers, game developers, educators, policymakers, youth experts, and others convened for an exploration of the forces shaping online game communities and the impact of antisocial interactions on players ages 8-13.

This [report](https://clalliance.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/09/Raising-Good-Gamers-Envisioning-an-Agenda-for-Diversity-Inclusion-and-Fair-Play.pdf) from the Connected Learning Alliance synthesizes recommendations focused on cultivating empathetic, compassionate, and civically engaged youth in gaming spaces.

### [Teach Writing with the New English Language Arts Pack](https://education.minecraft.net/blog/teach-writing-with-the-new-english-language-arts-pack)

Check out the new English Language Arts Minecraft Pack created in partnership with the [National Writing Project](https://www.nwp.org/). These 10 lessons for Minecraft: Education Edition focus on world-building and engage students in game-based learning to learn about the writing process.
