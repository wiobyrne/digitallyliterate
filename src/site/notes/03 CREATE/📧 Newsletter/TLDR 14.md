Over the past couple of TL;DR issues I've shared some research and posts discussing what our brains "see" while reading. In this post from ** ArsTechnica (http://arstechnica.com/)

** This is what the Internet looks like (http://www.wired.com/2015/09/internet-looks-like-irl/)
