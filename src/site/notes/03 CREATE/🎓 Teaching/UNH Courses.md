## Notes on Course Variants

Several courses cover overlapping terrain and were taught under different numbers at different times:

- **ED 657**, **ED 6671**, and **ED 7710** are all variations of the same media literacy / new literacies course, adapted for different audiences and semesters.
- **EDDUC 7724** (taught by Shane A. Smith) is a section variant of **[[03 CREATE/🎓 Teaching/Courses/IT&DML/ED 7724 - Adaptive Technologies for the Inclusive Classroom\|ED 7724 - Adaptive Technologies for the Inclusive Classroom]]**.
- **COMM 7728** is a section variant of **[[03 CREATE/🎓 Teaching/Courses/IT&DML/CO 7728 - Online Content Construction and Remixing\|CO 7728 - Online Content Construction and Remixing]]**.
