# Subscribe to Digitally Literate

Digitally Literate is a weekly letter about the forces shaping how we read,
write, learn, and live in digital spaces.

Issues move across artificial intelligence, education, literacy, privacy,
platforms, and the open web. I use the letter to share what I am reading, work
through an argument, and connect current events to questions that deserve more
time.

[Subscribe on Substack](https://digitallyliterate.substack.com/)

You can unsubscribe at any time. If you prefer to read on the web, the
[complete archive](/newsletter/) remains open without a subscription.

Questions or delivery problems? Email
[hello@digitallyliterate.net](mailto:hello@digitallyliterate.net).
