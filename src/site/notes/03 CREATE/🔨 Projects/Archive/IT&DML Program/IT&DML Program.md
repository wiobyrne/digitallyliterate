# IT&DML Program

The Instructional Technology & Digital Media Literacy (IT&DML) program at the University of New Haven. A graduate program for educators designed around three tenets: new/digital/web literacies research, critical literacy, and open practice.

Developed and launched ~2012. 11-course sequence built on Chromebooks and Google Apps for Education, with students working openly online.
