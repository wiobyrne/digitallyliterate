# SC Teacher Certification Navigation Guide

*Comprehensive guide to South Carolina teacher certification pathways*

---

## Overview

South Carolina requires multiple assessments for initial teacher certification. This guide covers the pathway for elementary education candidates, with notes for other certification areas.

---

## Required Assessments

### 1. Praxis Core Academic Skills

Basic skills test required before admission to teacher education programs.

| Test | Code | Passing Score |
|------|------|---------------|
| Reading | 5713 | 156 |
| Writing | 5723 | 162 |
| Mathematics | 5733 | 150 |

**Combined Test Option:** Praxis Core Academic Skills for Educators (5752) - can take all three in one sitting.

**Registration:** [ETS Praxis Website](https://www.ets.org/praxis)

**Study Resources:**
- Khan Academy (free, aligned to Praxis Core)
- ETS official practice tests
- Praxis Core prep books (Kaplan, Barron's)

---

### 2. Praxis Subject Assessments

Content knowledge tests specific to your certification area.

**Elementary Education:**
| Test | Code | Passing Score |
|------|------|---------------|

| - Reading & Language Arts | 5902 | 157 |
| - Mathematics | 5903 | 157 |
| - Social Studies | 5904 | 155 |

**Other Common Areas:**

- Middle Level Mathematics (5164)

### 3. Principles of Learning and Teaching (PLT)

Pedagogical knowledge assessment.

| Grade Level | Code | Passing Score |
|-------------|------|---------------|

| PLT: K-6 | 5622 | 160 |
| PLT: 5-9 | 5623 | 160 |
| PLT: 7-12 | 5624 | 157 |

---

### 4. Performance Assessment

South Carolina accepts either edTPA or PPAT for the performance assessment requirement.

#### edTPA
- Portfolio-based assessment
- Completed during student teaching
- Includes video of instruction
- Scored by trained evaluators

**Components:**
1. Planning (Task 1)
2. Instruction (Task 2)
3. Assessment (Task 3)

**Passing Score:** 37 (elementary education)

#### PPAT (Praxis Performance Assessment for Teachers)
- Alternative to edTPA
- Four tasks completed during student teaching
- Similar structure to edTPA

**Tasks:**
1. Knowledge of Students and the Learning Environment
2. Assessment and Data Collection
3. Designing Instruction for Student Learning
4. Implementing and Analyzing Instruction

## Recommended Timeline

### Year 1-2 (Freshman/Sophomore)
- [ ] Complete Praxis Core before applying to teacher education program
- [ ] Begin building content knowledge

### Year 3 (Junior)
- [ ] Take Praxis Subject Assessments
- [ ] Retake any failed tests (allow 28 days between attempts)

### Year 4 (Senior/Student Teaching)
- [ ] Complete edTPA or PPAT during student teaching
- [ ] Take PLT if not already completed
- [ ] Submit application for initial certification

## Registration and Scores

### How to Register
1. Create an ETS account at [ets.org/praxis](https://www.ets.org/praxis)
2. Select your test(s) and testing date
3. Choose score recipients (include SC Department of Education)

### Score Reporting
- Scores typically available 10-16 days after testing
- Official scores sent to institutions you designate
- SC Department of Education code: 8099

### Retake Policy
- Must wait 28 days between attempts of the same test
- No limit on number of attempts
- Must pay full fee for each attempt

## Study Strategies

### For Praxis Core
- Use Khan Academy's free Praxis prep
- Take official ETS practice tests under timed conditions
- Focus on areas of weakness identified in practice tests

### For Subject Assessments
- Review your program's content courses
- Use ETS study companions (free)
- Form study groups with peers
