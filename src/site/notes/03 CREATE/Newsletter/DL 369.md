# DL 369

## Plagiarize The Brain

Welcome to _Digitally Literate_, issue #369. This week, we reflect on resilience, the future of AI, and the changing role of libraries. Let’s dive in.

---

## 🔖 Key Takeaways
- **The Far Future**: Understanding the trajectory from ANI to AGI and ASI prepares us for the profound changes ahead.
- **Libraries and Literacy**: Libraries remain essential to social infrastructure, evolving to meet modern challenges.
- **Parenting and Social Media**: Balance and communication are key to supporting teens' mental health and digital habits.

---

## 📚 Readings & Insights

### 1. **Cormac McCarthy and _The Road_**
This week, we lost Cormac McCarthy, author of _The Road_. His love for his son inspired this haunting and hopeful tale of survival.

**Why this matters:** McCarthy's works remind us to find meaning and resilience even in the darkest times.

---

### 2. **America’s Declining School-Age Population**
The shrinking school-age population could lead to closures and consolidations, impacting educational quality and accessibility.

**Why this matters:** Amid culture wars, we must focus on long-term solutions for sustaining public education.

---

### 3. **The Vital Role of Libraries**
Summer reading challenges with my kids highlight how libraries evolve alongside our literacy practices. Yet, these institutions face growing demands and challenges.

**Why this matters:** Supporting libraries ensures they remain vital hubs for learning and community.

---

### 4. **Teens and Social Media**
While social media's effects on teens are debated, balancing screen time with other activities is crucial. Setting limits, modeling responsible use, and fostering dialogue can help.

**Why this matters:** Engaging with teens about their digital habits fosters healthier relationships with technology.

---

### 5. **The Far Future and AI Levels**
Tim Urban explores the leap from ANI to AGI and ASI, forecasting unprecedented change by 2025.

**Why this matters:** Our ability to envision and prepare for transformative AI will shape societal outcomes. [Learn more](https://www.example.com).

---

### 6. **Implementing Change Effectively**
Rather than convincing everyone at once, focus on early adopters to build momentum and demonstrate success.

**Why this matters:** Success breeds more success, turning small wins into transformative movements.

---

### 7. **Reframing Experiences for Happiness**
Chris Bailey highlights how perception influences happiness more than the events themselves. Shifting our mindset can improve overall satisfaction.

**Why this matters:** Cultivating a positive outlook empowers us to navigate life’s challenges with resilience.

---

## 🛠️ DO: Build Resilience in Children

Teaching adaptability and resourcefulness prepares children to face diverse challenges. Focus on:
- Problem-solving skills.
- Emotional regulation.
- Encouraging curiosity and independence.
