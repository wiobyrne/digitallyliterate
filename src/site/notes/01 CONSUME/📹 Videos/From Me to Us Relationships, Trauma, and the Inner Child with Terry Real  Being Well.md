## Summary
What gets in the way of truly intimate, healthy relationships\u2026and what can we do about it? Renowned couples therapist @realterryreal joins me to explore how we can get past our conditioning and build deeper, more meaningful connections. Part of the episode is an experiential process where Terry guides me through some inner child work, it's probably the most revealed I've been on the show. Let me know what you think.

About our Guest: Terry Real is a family therapist, speaker, and bestselling author known for his groundbreaking work on men, masculinity, and relationships. He is the founder of Relational Life Therapy (RLT) and the author of several bestselling books.
