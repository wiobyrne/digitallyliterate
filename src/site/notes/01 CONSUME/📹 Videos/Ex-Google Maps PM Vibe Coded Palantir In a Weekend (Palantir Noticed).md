## Summary

Live satellite tracking, military aircraft via ADS-B, real-time CCTV projected onto 3D geometry, seismic data, and street traffic; all running against Google's 3D tiles and skinned with CRT, night vision, and FLIR filters. Basically what happens when Google Earth and Palantir have a baby.
