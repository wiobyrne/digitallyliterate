# Trust and Sincerity Detection in AI

*Using AI to identify trustworthiness of online information*

> Trust, sincerity, and authenticity in online information are increasingly hard to discern.

---

## The Concept

An AI system designed to identify the trust and sincerity level of information encountered online.

**Form factor:** Browser extension

**Analysis includes:**
- Message content, tone, and style
- IP address signals
- Data patterns of incoming messages
- Potential vectors of attack

## Design Considerations

### What to Analyze
- Linguistic patterns indicating deception
- Metadata inconsistencies
- Source reputation signals
- Behavioral anomalies

### User Experience
- Non-intrusive indicators
- Clear explanations of findings
- Actionable recommendations
- Privacy-preserving design

### Ethical Considerations
- Avoiding false positives that silence legitimate voices
- Transparency about how determinations are made
- User control over sensitivity levels
- Not creating new forms of surveillance
