# Computational Thinking as Communication

*From code to conversation in K-8 classrooms*

> AI-powered coding tools do not merely serve as neutral learning aids; rather, they mediate and transform language use, meaning-making, and social interactions.

---

## Key Concepts

### AI Prompting as Communicative Act
In environments where students co-create with LLMs or generative agents, prompts function as:
- Iterative, audience-aware messages
- Drawing on decomposition, pattern recognition, and algorithmic logic
- Dialogic, metacommunicative processes

Students use natural and structured language to "speak" to AI and interpret its responses.

### Collaborative Sense-Making with AI
Learners co-construct code, text, and meaning with AI, reshaping:
- Literate identity
- Agency
- Cognitive frameworks

### Educational Tools as Epistemic Mediators
AI tools shape cognitive and communicative development in fundamental ways. They're not neutralâ€”they actively mediate how students think and express.
