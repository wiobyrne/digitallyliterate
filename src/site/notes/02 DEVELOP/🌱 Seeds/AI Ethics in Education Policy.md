## What This Means in Practice

### Encouraged
- Experiment with generative AI (ChatGPT, Claude, NotebookLM, etc.) as part of learning
- Use AI to draft, summarize, brainstorm, or review work

### Required
- **Disclose** when and how you use AI in assignments or projects
- **Reflect** on your experience with these tools

### Protected
- You will **not be penalized** for using AI as long as use is:
  - Transparent
  - Aligned with assignment goals
  - Reflective of your own thinking

### Expected
- Participate in discussions about broader implications of generative AI
- Consider impacts on education, equity, creativity, and knowledge production
