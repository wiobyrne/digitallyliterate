# Digital Sovereignty Framework

*Taking back control of your digital life*

> Our goal is to empower everyday people and communities to take back control of their digital lives.

---

## Core Principles (Plain English)

### Privacy by Design
Building and using tools that **protect your data by default**. Privacy isn't an afterthoughtâ€”it's baked in from the start.

In practice: Choose apps that minimize data collection and use strong encryption automatically. *You* decide who sees your information, not the platform.

### Data/Digital Sovereignty
Having **control over your own digital data and how it's used**.

Think of it like owning your home instead of renting. You're not stuck with a "landlord" (big company) who can change the rules. You and your community choose services that serve *your* interests.

### Digital Resilience
The ability to **keep digital systems running smoothly even when things go wrong**.

Building redundancy, independence, and adaptability into your digital life so you can keep communicating and organizing even when platforms fail.

---

## Why This Matters

Most of us rely on big tech platforms that make money from our data (*surveillance capitalism*). This leaves us feeling exposed and powerless.

**The alternative:** Tools and approaches that put privacy, community control, and resilience first. By understanding a few core concepts, we can make smarter choices that protect our personal and community information.
