# Social Emotional Learning

*Learning to understand ourselves and connect with others*

> SEL is about helping people understand and manage their emotions, build healthy relationships, and make good decisions.

---

## What is SEL?

Social and Emotional Learning (SEL) teaches skills for:
- Recognizing and handling feelings
- Understanding and caring for others
- Making responsible choices
- Working well with others

**In simple terms:** SEL helps us become more aware of our emotions, communicate better, and treat others with respect â€” so we can get along with people and succeed in life.

---

## The Five Core Competencies

### 1. Self-Awareness
- Recognizing your emotions
- Understanding your strengths and limitations
- Having a sense of confidence and purpose

### 2. Self-Management
- Regulating emotions and behaviors
- Managing stress
- Setting and working toward goals
- Showing self-discipline

### 3. Social Awareness
- Understanding others' perspectives
- Empathizing with diverse backgrounds
- Recognizing social norms
- Identifying resources and supports

### 4. Relationship Skills
- Communicating clearly
- Listening actively
- Cooperating with others
- Resolving conflicts constructively
- Seeking and offering help

### 5. Responsible Decision-Making
- Considering ethics, safety, and social norms
- Evaluating consequences of actions
- Making constructive choices about behavior

---

## Why SEL Matters

| Domain | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Academic** | Better focus, engagement, and achievement |
| **Behavioral** | Fewer discipline issues, less aggression |
| **Social** | Improved relationships, collaboration |
| **Emotional** | Better mental health, less anxiety |
| **Long-term** | Career success, civic engagement |

---

## SEL in Practice

**For educators:**
- Model emotional regulation
- Create safe spaces for expression
- Teach vocabulary for emotions
- Practice conflict resolution
- Integrate SEL into content areas

**For individuals:**
- Practice naming emotions
- Develop pause-and-reflect habits
- Seek to understand before being understood
- Set and track personal goals

---

## Connection to Digital Life

In digital spaces, SEL skills become even more important:
- Managing reactions to online content
- Empathizing across screens
- Making responsible choices about sharing
- Building healthy online relationships
- Recognizing manipulation and harm
