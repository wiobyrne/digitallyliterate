# Digitally Literate 🌱
My digital garden—an evolving repository of thoughts, ideas, and resources designed to learn in public and cultivate knowledge.

# Purpose
This digital garden serves as a space to:
- Share my knowledge and ideas publicly.
- Explore topics related to digital literacy, creativity, and professional development.
- Connect with others and inspire learning and growth.

In the future, this repository will power my website, [DigitallyLiterate.net](https://digitallyliterate.net/), where I will:

- Publish my newsletter.
- Showcase connections between ideas.
- Offer professional development services and speaking opportunities.

# Features
- **Digital Garden Structure**: Notes are interconnected and categorized as seeds, plants, or evergreen content to reflect their maturity.
- **Topics Covered**: Digital literacy, technology, creativity, education, and more.
- **Open Knowledge Sharing**: Licensed under CC BY 4.0.
